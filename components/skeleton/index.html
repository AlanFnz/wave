Skeleton

Provide a placeholder while you wait for content to load, or to visualise content that doesn't exist yet.

Properties

NameTypeDefault
animated

boolean

Add shimmer animation to component		false

The Skeleton supports layout, margin and borderRadius styled-system props.

Examples

<Skeleton width="200px" animated />
